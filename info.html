What Do We Wear?

When selecting clothing for the session, solid colors are highly recommended. It is best to avoid busy stripes, prints, bold patterns, and logos. Trendy colors can date your portrait and are best avoided as well. Black and pastel colors work well in black and white portraits. Also keep in mind that long sleeves are most flattering for adults. Shoes and socks should be similar tone to the outfit. With children, I like to do bare feet pictures instead of shoes. But, you can bring along coordinating shoes if you like. Simply avoid shoes with words and designs on the bottom, especially for infants not yet walking. For family portraits, it's best to dress everyone in the same style, color or mood. All light or all dark tones will make the image most successful. Several shades of one color can be nice. Also, simple tan pants or jeans and black shirts are great for a casual family photo. Bring a variety of outfits with you and feel free to make as many clothing changes as you like.

Remember, this is YOUR session!!

Working With Children

If you are scheduling a portrait session for your child, It helps to make sure your child gets plenty of sleep the night before the session, that nap time isn't too close to session time and they are well fed.

This should be FUN! Don't forget to bring favorite toys and blankets the day of the session. It is great to bring along your child's favorite items that make them feel comfortable and bring out their personality.
